What it is

Wound care for the post-surgical pathway

Surgical dressings cover and support healing of surgical and certain other wounds. Different chemistries do different jobs: collagen dressings (and powder) support the wound bed; composite island dressings combine an absorbent pad with an adhesive border; silicone composite dressings add a gentler, atraumatic contact layer. The right product and size depend on the wound and the care plan.

These items are billed under Local Coverage Determination L33831 (Surgical Dressings). There is no prior-authorization requirement and no Written Order Prior to Delivery requirement — coverage turns on the order and the medical-record documentation supporting medical necessity, with the usual quantity and frequency expectations under L33831.
