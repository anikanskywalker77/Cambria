What it is

Trunk and lumbar-sacral support, by indication

A spinal orthosis is a brace that supports the thoracic, lumbar, or sacral spine — used for conditions like post-surgical stabilization, compression fractures, and degenerative disease. The right device depends on the levels involved and how much rigidity the clinician wants: a flexible thoraco-lumbo-sacral wrap (TLSO) is a different order from a rigid lumbar-sacral shell (LSO).

All of these codes are governed by Local Coverage Determination L33790 (Spinal Orthoses: TLSO and LSO). Importantly, the rigid lumbar-sacral codes L0648, L0650, and L0651 are on the Medicare Required Prior Authorization List and require a Written Order Prior to Delivery — and L0651's prior-authorization requirement is new, effective April 13, 2026 (CMS-6097-N). We handle the prior-authorization submission and won't deliver until there's an affirmative decision and the WOPD is in hand.

Codes, products & requirements

L0651 changed in 2026. Effective 2026-04-13 (CMS-6097-N, 91 FR 1250), L0651 was added to the Required Prior Authorization List. Orders for L0651 now follow the same prior-authorization-then-WOPD path as L0648 and L0650. Our order forms were updated for this.

What "WOPD" means in practice: for L0648 / L0650 / L0651 we must have the complete, signed written order before the brace is delivered — not after. Get the order to us first and there's no delay; deliver-then-document isn't an option for these codes.

Pick the code

Choose by indication and rigidity — TLSO vs. LSO, flexible vs. rigid, the levels involved. Not sure which Trend product maps to your intended code? Call us.

Complete the SWO

Open the order form above, fill in patient/prescriber/diagnosis sections, mark the HCPCS, and have the treating practitioner sign. Face-to-face must be within the prior six months.

We open the prior auth

For codes that require it (L0648 / L0650 / L0651), we submit the prior-authorization request and don't furnish the device until there's an affirmative decision.

Fit, deliver & archive

We fit the brace to the patient, deliver it, and archive the order and supporting documentation — indexed by NPI and date.
